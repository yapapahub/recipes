# Alfredo Sauce 
(439 Calories)

## Ingridients:  
- 1/4 cup butter;  
- 1 cup heavy cream;  
- 1 clove garlic, crushed

## Instruction:  
* Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add cream and simmer 
for 5 minutes, then add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating 
through. Stir in parsley and serve.
